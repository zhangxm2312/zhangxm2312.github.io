## 文本

> $$A_+ A_− = A_− A_+ = 0, A(G) = A_+ − A_−.$$

后者是显然的,下证前者.因为
$$A_+A_-=\br{\sum_{i\in I}\lambda_i x_i x_i^T}\br{\sum_{j\in J}\lambda_j x_j x_j^T}=\sum_{i,j} \lambda_i \lambda_j x_i x_i$$
